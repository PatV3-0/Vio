Meet   Patterson

Patterson Rainbird-Webb is an aspiring artist and developer currently studying their second degree.

They listen to heavy metal and watch horror, but they also have a soft spot for mustelids, growing things and their pet cat Brylee whom everyone refers to as "The Cheese". They have a habit of collecting hobies and are currently interested in nuclear fision and welding... No connection at all, we swear.

They hope that one day they can use all of the hobies they have collected to make the world a cooler and more accessible place and their current goal is to make video games that people with photosensitive epilepsy can play.

Some very Patterson things

Here are some interesting things about them:

  • They come with their own sound effects
  • Their cat, Brylee, is a therapy animal
  • Their favourite comic artist is ND Stevenson
  • If they could have a super power, they'd control electricity
  • Their dad is a science teacher who loves space almost as much as they do and they have matching scars from a lab (kitchen) accident
  • You can ALWAYS bribe them with sour sweets

After losing her dream job and a horrific fallout with her partner, Jamie Birch finds a run-down house in the countryside where she can be alone and do some work to take her mind off things. But when she arrives, she finds that the house isn't as empty as it looks.

Nate has lived in the house for longer than he can remember, kept company by only his kitten and a few other ghosts that come and go. But Jamie's arrival sparks a new excitement and before they know it, the old house is impossibly teeming with new life.

About the author

This comic is a student project by Patterson Rainbird-Webb, who, when they aren't working to get better at coding and animation, enjoys baking, listening to metal and coming up with new stories and games.
